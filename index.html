About Me

I am a dedicated and passionate bioinformatician with a Master of Technology from Delhi Technological University. My core ambition is to drive innovation in precision medicine. I thrive in collaborative environments where I can apply my integrated expertise to make impactful scientific discoveries.

What truly defines my approach is the synergy I create between wet lab experimentation, complex omics landscapes, and advanced machine learning. I don't just analyze data; I understand its biological origins and context. This allows me to not only uncover novel molecular mechanisms but also to conceptualize and develop targeted therapeutic strategies. I am deeply interested in exploring the intricate genetic, molecular, and cellular determinants of disease, including the interplay of microenvironments, aging, and signaling processes.
